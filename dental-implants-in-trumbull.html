Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Implants

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry of Trumbull, and does insurance cover any of the expenses?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, the cost for a single dental implant usually ranges from $3,500 to $5,500, depending on the complexity of your case and any additional procedures you may need, such as bone grafting. Some dental insurance plans in Connecticut may partially cover the procedure, especially the restoration (crown) part, but coverage varies widely. Our office staff can review your insurance benefits and provide a detailed estimate before treatment begins.
What is the typical timeline for getting a dental implant at your Trumbull office?
The process for getting a dental implant at GD Dentistry of Trumbull usually takes several months. After an initial consultation and planning, the implant is surgically placed and allowed to heal and integrate with the bone for 3 to 6 months. Once healing is complete, we place the abutment and the final crown. If you need any preparatory procedures like bone grafting, it may add a few more months to the timeline. We’ll provide you with a personalized treatment schedule during your consultation.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing dental implant procedures?
Absolutely. All of our dentists at GD Dentistry of Trumbull are fully licensed in the state of Connecticut and have received specialized training in implant dentistry. We regularly participate in continuing education to stay up-to-date with the latest techniques and technology, ensuring safe and effective care for our patients.
What can I expect during the dental implant procedure at GD Dentistry of Trumbull?
The process begins with a comprehensive evaluation, including 3D imaging to assess your bone structure. On the day of surgery, we use local anesthesia to ensure your comfort. The dental implant is placed into your jawbone, and you may receive a temporary restoration while the area heals. You’ll have follow-up visits so we can monitor healing, and once the implant has integrated, we’ll place the final crown. Our team will guide you through each step and answer any questions you have along the way.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on dental implants at your practice?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, we stand by the quality of our dental implant work. We offer a 5-year warranty on the implant itself, provided you maintain regular check-ups and proper oral hygiene. If any issues arise due to material defects or our workmanship during this period, we will address them at no additional cost. We’ll discuss all warranty details and expectations with you during your consultation.
