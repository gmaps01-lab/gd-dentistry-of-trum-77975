Frequently Asked Questions About Root Canal Therapy

How much does a root canal cost at GD Dentistry of Trumbull, and does insurance usually cover it?
The cost of a root canal at GD Dentistry of Trumbull typically ranges from $900 to $1,400, depending on which tooth needs treatment and the complexity of the procedure. Most dental insurance plans in Connecticut cover a significant portion of root canal therapy, but coverage varies. Our team is happy to provide a personalized estimate and check your insurance benefits for you before your appointment.
How long does the root canal process take, and will I need more than one visit?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, most root canal treatments are completed in one to two visits, each lasting about 60 to 90 minutes. The exact number of visits depends on the severity of the infection and the tooth involved. We'll let you know at your initial consultation what to expect for your specific case.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing root canals in Connecticut?
Absolutely. All our dentists are licensed by the Connecticut State Dental Board and have extensive training and experience in endodontic procedures, including root canal therapy. We use the latest techniques and technology to ensure your treatment is safe, effective, and comfortable.
What is the step-by-step process for getting a root canal at GD Dentistry of Trumbull?
Your root canal treatment begins with a thorough exam and digital X-rays to assess the infection. We’ll numb the area with local anesthesia to keep you comfortable. The dentist will carefully remove the infected pulp, clean and disinfect the inside of the tooth, and then fill and seal it. In most cases, we recommend a follow-up appointment for a dental crown to protect the tooth long-term.
Does GD Dentistry of Trumbull offer any guarantees or follow-up care after a root canal?
We stand by the quality of our work and provide detailed aftercare instructions following your root canal. Our team offers a complimentary follow-up exam within six months to ensure proper healing. If you experience any complications, please contact us right away—we’re committed to your comfort and the long-term success of your treatment.
