Frequently Asked Questions About Emergency Dental Care

What qualifies as a dental emergency, and how quickly can I be seen at GD Dentistry of Trumbull?
Dental emergencies include severe toothaches, knocked-out or broken teeth, abscesses, swelling, and injuries to your gums or mouth. At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, we prioritize emergency cases and strive to offer same-day appointments whenever possible. Call our office immediately, and our team will advise you on next steps and work to fit you in as quickly as possible.
How much does an emergency dental visit cost at your Trumbull office, and do you accept my insurance?
The cost of an emergency dental visit at GD Dentistry of Trumbull typically ranges from $100 to $250, depending on the type of treatment needed. This fee covers the exam and any necessary X-rays. We accept most major dental insurance plans and can provide an estimate of your out-of-pocket costs after verifying your coverage. For patients without insurance, we offer flexible payment options and can discuss costs before treatment begins.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in handling dental emergencies?
Yes, all of our dentists at GD Dentistry of Trumbull are fully licensed in Connecticut and have extensive experience managing a wide range of dental emergencies. Our team regularly completes continuing education courses and uses the latest techniques to ensure you receive prompt, effective care.
What should I expect during an emergency dental visit at GD Dentistry of Trumbull?
When you arrive for an emergency visit, our team will quickly assess your situation, take any necessary X-rays, and discuss your symptoms. The dentist will explain your treatment options and answer any questions you have. Many emergencies can be treated during the same visit, but some cases may require follow-up appointments. We focus on relieving your pain first, then addressing the underlying issue.
Do you guarantee your emergency dental work, and what should I do if I have issues after my visit?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, we stand by the quality of our emergency dental care. If you experience any issues or persistent discomfort after your treatment, please contact us right away.
