Frequently Asked Questions About Implant Dentist

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry of Trumbull, and does insurance usually cover them?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, the cost for a single dental implant—including the implant itself, abutment, and crown—generally ranges from $3,500 to $5,000 depending on the complexity and type of restoration required. Some dental insurance plans in Connecticut may cover portions of the procedure, such as the crown, but most do not cover the full implant process. We recommend bringing your insurance information to your consultation, so our team can provide a detailed estimate and help you understand any benefits or financing options available.
What is the typical timeline for getting a dental implant from start to finish?
The dental implant process at our Trumbull office usually takes three to six months from your initial consultation to the placement of the final crown. The timeline can vary depending on individual healing rates and whether you require procedures like bone grafting. After the implant is surgically placed, a healing period of three to four months is typical before attaching the permanent crown, ensuring the implant fully integrates with your jawbone for long-term stability.
Is GD Dentistry of Trumbull licensed and experienced in implant dentistry?
Yes, GD Dentistry of Trumbull is fully licensed in Connecticut, and our dentists have advanced training and years of hands-on experience in implant dentistry. We stay current with the latest implant technologies and techniques, and all procedures are performed in-house at our Trumbull location to ensure the highest standards of care and safety.
What can I expect during the dental implant process at GD Dentistry of Trumbull?
The implant process begins with a comprehensive consultation and 3D imaging to assess your jawbone and discuss your goals. On the day of surgery, the implant post is placed under local anesthesia. After a healing period (osseointegration), you’ll return for abutment placement and, finally, the custom crown. We offer sedation options for comfort and provide detailed aftercare instructions. Throughout, our Trumbull team is available to answer questions and monitor your healing.
Does GD Dentistry of Trumbull offer any guarantees or warranties on dental implants?
We stand behind the quality of our implant work and provide a limited warranty on both the implant and restoration, provided patients maintain recommended oral hygiene and attend regular check-ups at our Trumbull office. If issues arise related to materials or workmanship within the warranty period, we will address them at no additional charge. We’ll review all warranty details with you during your treatment planning.
