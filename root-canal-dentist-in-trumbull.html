Frequently Asked Questions About Root Canal Dentist

How much does a root canal typically cost at GD Dentistry of Trumbull, and do you accept dental insurance?
The cost of a root canal at GD Dentistry of Trumbull usually ranges from $950 to $1,400, depending on the complexity and the specific tooth involved. We accept most major dental insurance plans and can help you estimate your out-of-pocket costs before treatment. For patients without insurance, we offer flexible payment options and CareCredit financing. We always provide a detailed treatment plan and cost breakdown during your initial consultation.
How long does a root canal procedure take, and will I need multiple visits?
Most root canal treatments at our Trumbull office can be completed in one or two visits, each lasting about 60 to 90 minutes. The exact number of visits depends on the condition of your tooth and whether there is an active infection. Our dentist will assess your specific case and discuss the timeline with you during your first appointment.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing root canals?
Yes, all of our dentists at GD Dentistry of Trumbull are fully licensed in Connecticut and have extensive training in endodontic (root canal) procedures. Our team regularly attends continuing education courses to stay current with the latest techniques and technologies. You can feel confident that your care is in experienced hands.
What is the process for getting a root canal at your Trumbull office?
The process begins with a consultation, where we take digital X-rays and perform an exam to confirm you need a root canal. On the day of the procedure, we numb the area thoroughly to ensure your comfort. The dentist removes the infected pulp, cleans and shapes the canal, and then fills and seals the tooth. In most cases, we recommend placing a crown afterwards to protect the tooth, which may require a follow-up visit. We will explain every step and answer your questions along the way.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on your root canal treatments?
At GD Dentistry of Trumbull, we stand by the quality of our work. We offer a one-year warranty on root canal treatments, provided you follow recommended care instructions and attend your regular check-ups. If you experience any issues related to the treated tooth within that time, please contact us right away so we can address your concerns promptly.
